# Kaya AI Assistant

**Kaya** is your built-in AI Project Manager, designed to automate the tedious parts of coordination and give you deep insights into your team's velocity and workload.

Available exclusively on the **Pro Plan**, Kaya lives in your workspace as a persistent, intelligent partner.

## How to Access Kaya

You can interact with Kaya in three ways:
1.  **AI Workspace**: Click the **"AI Assistant"** tab in your project sidebar for a full-screen chat experience.
2.  **Floating Chat**: Use the Kaya bubble in the bottom right of your dashboard for quick questions.
3.  **Command Palette**: Press `Cmd+K` (or `Ctrl+K`) and type "Ask Kaya..." to trigger an instant query.

---

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Smart Sprint Planning
Kaya can analyze your backlog and propose a balanced sprint. It looks at:
-   **Task Estimates**: Total hours vs. team capacity.
-   **Priority**: Ensuring high-stakes work is handled first.
-   **Assignee Load**: Distributing tasks to prevent burnout.

> **Try it:** *"Kaya, help me plan a 2-week sprint with the highest priority tasks from the backlog."*

### 2. Automated Project Reports
Stop manually compiling status updates. Kaya can generate structured reports including:
-   **Completion Rate**: Percent of tasks finished vs. planned.
-   **Bottleneck Detection**: Identifying tasks that haven't moved in 3+ days.
-   **Activity Trends**: Who is making the most progress and where.

### 3. Schedulers & Reminders
Kaya can act as your personal assistant by managing your calendar and notifications.
-   **Set Reminders**: *"Remind the team to update their time logs every Friday at 4 PM."*
-   **Auto-Reports**: Schedule a "Monday Morning Briefing" to be sent to your team space.

### 4. Workload Insights (Heatmaps)
Kaya processes data from the **Heatmap** view to provide natural-language summaries of team health.
-   *"Alex is currently at 120% capacity; consider reassigning the 'API Refactor' task."*
-   *"The team velocity has increased by 15% since we started using Sprints."*

## Quick Actions Reference

In the AI Workspace, you'll find quick-action tiles to trigger common workflows:

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Project Report** | Generates a full analysis of your current project state. |
| **Set Reminder** | Creates a one-time or recurring event on your calendar. |
| **Create Sprint** | Launches the smart sprint planning wizard. |
| **Auto Schedulers** | Sets up automated reporting frequencies for your team. |

---

## Privacy & Context
Kaya only has access to the data within the project it is active in. It does not "see" your source code unless you explicitly paste snippets into the chat or use the VS Code extension's "Explain Code" feature.
