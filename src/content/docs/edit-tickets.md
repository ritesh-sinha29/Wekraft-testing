# Edit & Resolve Tickets

Learn how to view, reopen, and close active chat tickets.

---

## Where to Go & How to Go

### 1. Accessing Channel Tickets
- **Where to Go**: Navigate to **Teamspace** and select the chat channel where the ticket was logged.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Click the **Tickets** dropdown icon in the top header of the active channel.
  2. The dropdown lists all open tickets associated with the channel.
  3. You can toggle between the **Open** and **Closed** lists inside the dropdown.

### 2. Resolving and Closing a Ticket
- **Where to Go**: Inside the **Tickets** dropdown menu in the channel header.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Find the target ticket in the Open list.
  2. Click the status checkmark (or close action) next to the ticket.
  3. The status will transition from `open` to `closed` in the database, and the ticket will move to the Closed tickets archive.

### 3. Reopening a Closed Ticket
- **Where to Go**: Inside the **Tickets** dropdown menu in the channel header.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Click on the **Closed** tab/list.
  2. Locate the ticket you wish to reopen.
  3. Click the reopen action next to it.
  4. The ticket status transitions back to `open`, placing it back in the active queue.
