# Assign Sprint Items

Manage task assignments within sprints, balance workloads, and coordinate team commitments.

---

## Where to Go & How to Go

### 1. Assigning Sprint Tasks/Issues to Developers
- **Where to Go**: Open the sprint details page at **Manage → Sprint → [Sprint Name]** (or `/workspace/sprint/[sprintName]`).
- **How to Go**:
  1. In the sprint's item tables (Tasks or Issues tabs), click on any row to open the details side sheet.
  2. Locate the **Assignees** section and check/uncheck the team members responsible for this item.
  3. The sprint stats page will immediately update its **Assignees** listing to reflect the developers active on the sprint.

### 2. Reviewing Sprint commitments & Balances
- **Where to Go**: Navigate to the **Project Workspace Dashboard** (`/workspace`).
- **How to Go**:
  1. Open the **Advanced Charts** tab.
  2. Locate the **Member Workload Card**, which tracks the volume of active tasks and issues currently assigned to each team member, grouped by priority (`High`, `Medium`, `Low`).
  3. If a team member is overloaded, navigate to the active task list or sprint details, open the task, and modify the assignees to assign work to others.

---

## Sprint Allocation Guidelines

To maintain healthy sprint metrics and high team velocity:
1. Ensure tasks are fully assigned and estimated before starting the sprint.
2. Monitor member workloads in the workspace **Advanced Charts** to prevent developer burnout.
3. If a task becomes blocked during the sprint, escalate it using **Mark as Issue** to alert the team and automatically carry over assignees.
