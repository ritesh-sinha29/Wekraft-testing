# Kaya PM Agent

Meet **Kaya**, your AI-native Product Manager. Built deeply into the Wekraft platform, Kaya possesses the context of your entire organization and operates just like a human project manager—but at machine speed. 

Kaya takes the heavy lifting out of managing software projects, letting your team focus on shipping great products.

## What Can Kaya Do?

Kaya is designed to seamlessly handle everything a human PM does. From sprint planning to stakeholder reporting, she's got it covered.

### 1. Automated Sprint Planning
Kaya automatically evaluates your team's backlog, historical velocity, and current workloads to propose the optimal sprint plan. She understands dependencies and ensures that no engineer is overallocated.

### 2. Intelligent Reporting & Analytics
Gone are the days of manual status updates. Kaya continuously analyzes task progression, Git activity, and time logs to generate real-time reports. She can:
- Summarize sprint progress for stakeholders.
- Identify bottlenecks before they become blockers.
- Send automated end-of-week summaries.

### 3. Backlog Grooming & Triage
Kaya actively monitors your backlog. She tags incoming issues, assigns priorities based on user impact, and writes detailed user stories based on brief descriptions.

### 4. Full Workspace Management
Kaya has full operational control over the project workspace. She can automatically create new UI screens, draft and create tasks, and assign those tasks to the right developers based on their current workload.

### 5. Team Space Interaction
Kaya doesn't just work behind the scenes; she is a core part of your team. In the **Team Space**, if you mention **@Kaya**, she will read the context of your conversation and reply directly to you, just like any other human team member.

## How Kaya Works

Kaya acts as the central brain of your project workspace. She connects your tasks, Git repositories, and team communications into a single cohesive strategy.

- **Team Inputs (Tasks & Chat)** flows into the **Kaya PM Agent**.
- **Git Repositories (Commits & PRs)** also feed into **Kaya**.
- With this data, Kaya **Proposes Sprint Plans**, **Generates Status Reports**, and **Manages Backlog Grooming**.

## Getting Started

To invoke Kaya, simply open your **Team Space** or use the shortcut `Ctrl + K` to bring up the AI Assistant panel. You can talk to Kaya in natural language:

> *"Kaya, can you groom the backlog for the next release and assign high-priority bugs to the available developers?"*
