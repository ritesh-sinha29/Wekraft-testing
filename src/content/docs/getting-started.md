# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to WeKraft! This quickstart guide is structured directly around the **Getting Started** checklist on your dashboard. Completing these steps will take you from a fresh sign-up to a fully configured, collaborative, and AI-enabled workspace.

---

## The Onboarding Setup Checklist

Your dashboard tracks 7 key onboarding steps. Here is how to complete each one:

### Step 1: Connect your GitHub Account
- **Objective**: Link your developer profile to your GitHub account to enable sync features.
- **Action**: From the dashboard checklist, click **"Connect GitHub account"**. This opens the OAuth dialog. Once authenticated, your commits and pull requests can sync with the platform.

### Step 2: Link repository to your Project
- **Objective**: Attach a GitHub repository to synchronize file trees and code changes.
- **Action**: Click **"Link a repository"** on the checklist (or go to **Repositories** in the user menu). Select a connected GitHub repository and link it to your active project workspace.

### Step 3: Visit your Project workspace
- **Objective**: Tour the project layout and command center.
- **Action**: Click **"Go to workspace"** to enter the main developer dashboard.

### Step 4: Create your first task
- **Objective**: Populate the task list or backlog.
- **Action**: Click **"Go to Tasks"** on the checklist (or navigate to **Manage → Tasks** inside your workspace and click **"+ New Task"**). Add a title, priority level, estimation window, and assign it to yourself or team members.

### Step 5: Set a project deadline to Track
- **Objective**: Establish the delivery target date.
- **Action**: Click **"Open workspace overview"** on the checklist. Set a final delivery date for your project. This activates metrics tracking such as Milestone Trajectory and Delay Debt.

### Step 6: Invite teammates to collaborate
- **Objective**: Add team members up to your plan limit.
- **Action**: Click **"Invite teammates"** on the checklist (or click **"Invite"** in the header of the workspace). Copy the generated invite link and share it. When they open it, you can approve their join request under the **Requests** tab in your project dashboard.

### Step 7: Unlock your free trial (no charges)
- **Objective**: Experience premium features.
- **Action**: Click **"Unlock Trial"** on your dashboard checklist to activate a 1-week free trial of the Plus plan features without entering credit card details.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I have to complete Step 7 (Free Trial) to finish the onboarding?**
A: No. Step 7 is optional. Steps 1-6 are the required core steps. Once you finish steps 1-6, your checklist is officially marked complete, and the checklist is replaced by the **Notifications** panel.

**Q: Can I change my linked repository later?**
A: Yes, you can unlink and link a new repository from the **Repositories** dashboard view at any time.
