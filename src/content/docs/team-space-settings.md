# Team Space Settings

Configure channel details, manage membership lists, delete channels, and adjust member permission policies.

---

## Where to Go & How to Go

### 1. Modifying Channel Details
- **Where to Go**: Open **Teamspace**, then hover over the channel you wish to update in the channel list.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Click the three-dot options menu next to the channel name.
  2. Click **Edit Channel**.
  3. Modify the channel name or description as needed. (Note: Names of default channels, such as `#general`, are locked and cannot be edited).
  4. For private channels, project owners and admins can also manage the participant access list here by checking or unchecking members.
  5. Click **Save Changes**.

### 2. Deleting a Channel
- **Where to Go**: Hover over the channel in the Teamspace channel list.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Click the three-dot options menu next to the channel name.
  2. Click **Delete Channel**.
  3. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation prompt. The channel and all its message history will be permanently deleted.
  4. *Note: Default channels cannot be deleted.*

### 3. Adjusting Member Permissions (Settings Panel)
- **Where to Go**: Click the **Setting** button at the bottom of the Teamspace Channels sidebar.
- **How to Go**:
  1. A Teamspace Settings dialog will open.
  2. Project owners and admins can toggle the following permissions for regular team members:
     - **Create Channels**: Allow members to create new custom channels.
     - **Edit Channels**: Allow members to rename and edit descriptions of custom channels.
     - **Delete Channels**: Allow members to delete custom channels.
  3. Toggling a setting updates the permissions immediately for all project members. Regular members can view these settings but cannot toggle them.
