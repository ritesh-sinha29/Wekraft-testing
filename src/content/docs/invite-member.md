# Invite Member

Invite developers and collaborators to join your WeKraft project team.

---

## Where to Go & How to Go

### 1. Generating & Sharing the Project Invite Link
- **Where to Go**: Click on **Manage Team** in the project sidebar.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Click the **Invite** button in the header of the Team page to open the invitation dialog.
  2. Select the starting role for the invitee from the dropdown menu:
     - **Admin**: Full administrative permissions.
     - **Member**: Standard team member access.
     - **Viewer**: Read-only access.
  3. Click **Copy invite link** to copy the secure invitation URL to your clipboard.
  4. Share the link with your teammate. When they open it, they can request to join.

### 2. Inviting Members via Email
- **Where to Go**: In the **Invite** dialog on the **Manage Team** page.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Enter your teammate's email address in the input field.
  2. Select their role using the dropdown next to the input.
  3. Click the **Invite** button.
  4. An invitation email containing a secure link to join your project will be sent to the recipient.
  5. A toast notification will confirm that the invitation was sent successfully.

---

## Member Seat Limits

Each project has member limits based on the project owner's subscription plan:
- **Free Tier**: Maximum **3 members** total per project.
- **Plus Tier**: Maximum **6 members** total per project.
- **Pro Tier**: Maximum **15 members** total per project.

You can view current seats and availability in the plan banner at the top of the Team page. If you reach your limit, click **Upgrade to Pro** to invite more collaborators.
