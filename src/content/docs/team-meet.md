# Team Meet

The **Team Meet** feature is Wekraft's built-in real-time video collaboration space. It allows teammates to launch meetings, share screens, and brainstorm ideas directly from their project workspace without needing external meeting applications.

---

## Accessing Team Meet

To open the meeting portal:
1. Navigate to your project workspace.
2. Click the **Team Meet** tab in the project sidebar.
3. The main room dashboard will display, containing controls to schedule or launch meetings.

---

## Feature Roadmap & Capabilities

Team Meet is currently in active development as an upcoming premium tool:
- **Instant Video Conferencing**: Start one-click video rooms with low-latency audio/video feeds.
- **Screen Sharing**: Present document reviews, code files, or product demos to other members in the meeting room.
- **Meeting History**: Review past call timestamps, duration logs, and participant sheets to verify session logs.

---

## Subscription Requirements

Team Meet is designated as a **Pro Plan** feature:
- **Pro Tier Access**: Requires a valid Pro account for project owners to host meetings.
- **Trial Phase**: Free and Plus users can preview the dashboard layout but must upgrade their workspace subscription to initialize call rooms.
