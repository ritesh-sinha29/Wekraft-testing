# Create Channels

Create custom chat channels in WeKraft Teamspace to organize project discussions and coordinate feature implementations.

---

## Where to Go & How to Go

### Creating a New Channel
- **Where to Go**: Click on **Teamspace** in the sidebar.
- **How to Go**:
  1. If you have the required channel creation permissions, click the **Create Channel** button in the Channels sidebar.
  2. In the dialog, configure your new channel:
     - **Channel Type**: Choose **Community** (for general group chat) or **Announcement** (for broadcast-only channels).
     - **Private Channel**: If creating a Community channel, you can toggle **Private Channel** ON to restrict access. (Announcement channels cannot be made private).
     - **Add Members**: If Private Channel is enabled, select the checkboxes next to the project members you want to add to this channel.
     - **Channel Name**: Enter a short name. Names must contain only lowercase letters, numbers, spaces, or hyphens, and can be up to 32 characters long. (Any spaces will automatically be replaced with hyphens).
     - **Description**: Enter an optional description (up to 120 characters) explaining what the channel is for.
  3. Click **Create Channel**.
  4. The channel will be created instantly and will appear in the channel list for all authorized team members in real-time.

---

## Channel Types & Visibilities

| Channel Type | Who Can View & Join | Who Can Post | Use Cases |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Community Channel** | Any project member | All members | General discussions, Q&A, and updates. |
| **Announcement Channel**| Any project member | Owners and Admins only | Broadcast alerts, releases, and announcements. |
| **Private Channel** | Explicitly invited members | Invited members only | Confidential coordination, sub-teams, or private chats. |
