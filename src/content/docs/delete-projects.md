# Delete Projects

In WeKraft, manual deletion of projects is restricted to prevent accidental data loss and maintain database integrity.

---

## Where to Go & How to Go

### Requesting Project Deletion
- **Where to Go**: Click on **Delete Project** at the bottom of the left navigation sidebar.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Once on the Delete page, you will see an alert notice stating:
     > **You cannot delete this project manually. You need to raise a query related to this in Help and Support.**
  2. To initiate the deletion of your project, click on **Help and Support** located in the sidebar directly above the delete option.
  3. Submit a support query requesting project deletion. The support team will process your request and permanently remove the project and its resources.
