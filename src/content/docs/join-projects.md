# Join Projects

Search for public projects, submit access requests, and join collaborative team spaces.

---

## Where to Go & How to Go

### 1. Joining via Invitation Link
- **Where to Go**: Open the invitation link shared by your project administrator.
- **How to Go**:
  1. If you are not logged in, click **"Login to Continue"** to authenticate.
  2. Once authenticated, review the invitation details, which display the project name, the owner's details, and your assigned role.
  3. Click **"Accept & Join"**.
  4. An introduction dialog will prompt you to write an optional message to the project owner.
  5. Click **"Send Join Request"** to submit. Your request status will update to pending, and the owner will be notified to approve your request.

### 2. Requesting to Join Public Projects
- **Where to Go**: Visit a public project profile page.
- **How to Go**:
  1. Browse the public project profiles in the community portal.
  2. Click on a project to view its description, members list, tags, and repository statistics.
  3. Click **"Request to join"**.
  4. Your request is sent to the owner, and the status button updates to **"✓ Request in progress"**.

---

## Joined Projects Plan Limits

The number of projects you can join is limited based on your subscription tier:
- **Free Tier**: Maximum **2 joined projects**.
- **Plus Tier**: Maximum **10 joined projects**.
- **Pro Tier**: Maximum **20 joined projects**.

If a project has reached its seat capacity, the owner or admin cannot accept new members until the project plan is upgraded or an existing member is removed.
