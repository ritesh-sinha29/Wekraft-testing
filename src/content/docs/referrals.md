# Referral Program

The **Referral Program** allows you to earn rewards by inviting other users to join and onboard onto WeKraft. You can share your unique referral code and earn cash rewards or commissions.

---

## How It Works

Each user receives a unique referral code. When new users sign up and use your code during onboarding, they are recorded as your referral.

### Earning Rewards

You can earn rewards in several ways:
- **$2 for Every 10 Referrals**: For every 10 users who successfully onboard and complete the Getting Started tutorial using your code, you earn $2.
- **Max $10 Reward (50 Referrals)**: You can refer up to 50 users and earn a maximum of $10 in direct referral rewards.
- **20% Upgrade Commission**: If any of your referred users upgrade to a paid plan, you earn a straight 20% commission on their purchase.

---

## Accessing Your Referral Code

To view and copy your referral code:
1. Open the **Referral Dialog** from your Dashboard or Sidebar (you can access this by clicking on the Referral option).
2. Your unique referral code will be displayed.
3. Click **Copy Link** to share it with your network.

The dialog also displays your current referral progress, showing how many users you have referred (up to the 50 limit) and estimating your current referral earnings based on the $2 per 10 users rule.

---

## Using a Referral Code

If you were referred by someone else, you can enter their referral code during the **Onboarding** process in the "Source & Referral" step. The code must be in the valid format (typically `username-suffix`).
